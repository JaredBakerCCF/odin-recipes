To Die For Biscuits and Gravy Recipe

Description

Indulge your senses with the irresistible allure of freshly baked biscuits smothered in rich, savory gravy. Picture golden, flaky layers of biscuits, warm and inviting, ready to be generously coated in a velvety blend of creamy sausage-infused goodness. The enticing aroma and comforting flavors of this classic Southern dish promise a culinary embrace that will transport you to a world of culinary satisfaction, making it impossible to resist the urge to whip up a batch of Biscuits and Gravy immediately.

Ingredients

  • 1/3 Cup of Flour
  • Pillsbury Croissant (Butter Flake)
  • 3 Cups of Milk
  • 2 Tablespoons of Butter
  • Olive Oil
  • Jimmy Dean's Mild Sausage
  • 1/4 Teaspoon of Salt
  • Garlic Powder to Taste
  • 1 Tablespoon of Crushed Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Add croissants to a baking pan and set into oven at 375 Degrees Fahrenheit for 9-12 minutes or golden brown.
  2. Turn the stove on to medium-high heat.
  3. Once the pan has heated up (approx. 2 minutes), add 1 tablespoon of Olive Oil to the pan.
  4. Once the Olive Oil has heated up (approx. 2 minutes), add in the Sausage.
  5. Cook the Sausage until the meat is browned.
  6. When the meat is fully cooked, add in the butter and let it melt while stirring the meat around.
  7. After the butter has completely melted, sprinkle the flour on top of the cooked Sausage and mix well into the meat.
  8. Add in the milk and give it a quick mix, let the mixture come to a simmer.
  9. Add in Salt, Pepper, Garlic Powder.
  10. Slowly mix the seasoning in every minute or so as it continues to simmer.
  11. By now, the biscuits should be ready, take them out of the oven and prepare plating.
  12. Once the Sausage Gravy is complete, plate the biscuits and pour the gravy on top.
