Boss Level Prime Rib Recipe

Description

Elevate your holiday feast to extraordinary heights by conjuring the sumptuous magic of a perfectly cooked Prime Rib for Thanksgiving or Christmas. Envision a majestic roast, its exterior beautifully seared to a golden crust while the interior boasts a succulent, melt-in-your-mouth tenderness. The anticipation of carving into the juicy marbled slices, accompanied by the rich au jus, creates a festive symphony of flavors that will undoubtedly inspire you to make this show-stopping centerpiece a cherished tradition for your holiday celebrations.

Ingredients

  • Prime Rib
  • 2 Tbsp Rosemary
  • 2 Tbsp Thyme
  • 7 Cloves of Minced Garlic
  • 1.5 Tbsp Salt
  • 1 Tbsp of Black Pepper
  • 2 Sticks of Salted/Unsalted Butter

Steps

  1. Preheat oven for 550 Degrees
  2. Prepare a mixing bowl of all the spices necessary for the Prime Rib: Rosemary, Thyme, Minced Garlic, Salt, Pepper, and Butter
  3. Melt the butter and mix in the spices all together.
  4. Lather the Prime Rib with the buttery spice concoction.
  5. Place the Prime Rib bone down/Fat Up onto a pan that is able to catch the drippings (Can be saved later for Gravy).
  6. Place the Prime Rib into the oven for 5 minutes per pound of Prime Rib.
  7. Turn the oven off after the 5 minutes per pound time.
  8. Let the Prime Rib sit inside of the oven with it now off for 120 minutes.
  9. Allow a few minutes for the Prime Rib to rest when taken out of the oven and before serving.
  10. Cut and portion out Prime Rib pieces.
