Killer Katsu Curry Chicken Cutlet Recipe

Description

Embark on a culinary adventure with the tantalizing allure of homemade Katsu Curry Chicken Cutlet, reminiscent of the legendary flavors found at CoCo Ichiban's. Picture succulent, crispy chicken cutlets, perfectly breaded and golden brown, elegantly nestled atop a bed of fragrant Japanese curry sauce that's rich in aromatic spices and savory undertones. The harmonious blend of crispy textures and the robust curry notes promises a gastronomic journey that will leave you craving the authentic and comforting experience of CoCo Ichiban's, compelling you to recreate this delectable dish in your own kitchen without delay.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Salt
  • 0.5 Cups of Milk
  • 1 Tablespoons of Butter
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Chicken Breasts
  • 5 Blocks of Golden Curry
  • Garlic Powder to Taste
  • 1 Tablespoon of Crushed Black Pepper
  • Panko Crust
